# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to this interactive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This resource is designed to provide a comprehensive yet accessible introduction to the exciting field of robots that can perceive, learn, and interact with the physical world.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers the fundamental concepts and cutting-edge developments in Physical AI:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Understanding what makes Physical AI different from traditional AI
2. **Humanoid Robot Architecture** - The building blocks of humanoid robots
3. **Perception & Sensing** - How robots see, feel, and understand their environment
4. **Motion & Control** - Making robots move naturally and precisely
5. **Learning & Adaptation** - Teaching robots to improve through experience
6. **Applications & Future** - Real-world uses and what's coming next

## How to Use This Textbook

Each chapter is designed to be read in **under 7 minutes**, making it easy to learn at your own pace. The entire textbook can be completed in approximately **45 minutes**.

**Navigation tips:**
- Use the sidebar on the left to jump to any chapter
- Use the "Next" and "Previous" buttons at the bottom of each page
- On mobile, tap the menu icon to access the chapter list

## Who This Is For

This textbook is ideal for:
- Students curious about robotics and AI
- Professionals exploring the field of Physical AI
- Anyone interested in understanding how humanoid robots work

## Prerequisites

No prior robotics or AI experience is required. This textbook assumes only basic familiarity with technology concepts.
